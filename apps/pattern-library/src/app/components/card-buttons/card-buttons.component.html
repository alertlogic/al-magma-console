Card Buttons

Align Buttons Left

Place the button in an div with class="btnGroupLeft" and the div with the button will align to the left of its parent element.

Place class="left" on the button element to give the button a 6px left margin and a 0px right margin.

Align Buttons Right

Place the button in an div with class="btnGroupRight" and the div with the button will align to the right of its parent element.

Place class="right" on the button element to give the button a 6px left margin and a 0px right margin.
